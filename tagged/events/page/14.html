A one day festival celebrating the year of biodiversity with both workshops, talks, screenings and live performance. A big mix of things to do and to take active part of. Be there the whole day or only for an hour. Hope to see you there, here is the programme:

The Golden Hour: Literary Cabaret:

Join the gang from Edinburgh’s legendary Forest Cafe for a literary cabaret which has played to sold-out audiences in Berlin, Paris, Amsterdam, London and The Edinburgh Fringe Festival. It is poetry and prose, original eclectic songs + plenty of surprises. It is physical and mental. It is a reading. It is a gig. It is a party.

Featuring:

Readers / Writers:
Ryan Van Winkle – poems & stories from the Reader in Residence at the Scottish Poetry Library.
Ericka Duffy – Southern (Ontario) Gothic fiction & Poetry
Jason Morton – Shouts from the barroom floor.
+
Jane Flett – winner of the Scottish Book Trust New Writer’s Award and seamstress of many fetching stories

Music / Song Writers:
Hailey Beavis – an angelic voice, but devilish on six strings
Jed Milroy – bouncin’ bluegrass from the banjo balladeer
+
Taperecorder - weaves a shoegaze-crunk house hybrid as suited to cocooned winter days as to sweaty late-night adventuring.
